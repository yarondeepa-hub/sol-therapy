## WHAT NEEDS FIXING

### 1. Petition number (minor)
**Issue:** Blog says "כ-600 חוקרים" but verified number is ~544 in support.
**Fix:** Change to "למעלה מ-500 חוקרים" or keep "כ-600" with the understanding that total signers were 568.
**Severity:** Low - "about 600" is a common rounding in journalistic writing.

### 2. Dalai Lama quote placement (medium)
**Issue:** The blog structures it as: he spoke about secular ethics, "and then added a sentence that was etched in the history of both worlds." This implies the quote was part of the SfN speech. The famous quote "if science proves some belief of Buddhism wrong..." comes from his 2005 NYT op-ed "Our Faith in Science" published the same day. It may have also been said at SfN, but the primary verified source is the op-ed.
**Fix:** Either rephrase to "and in an op-ed he published that same day in the New York Times, he wrote:" or soften to "and around that time, he said a sentence that was etched..." This is important for factual accuracy.
**Severity:** Medium - this is exactly the kind of attribution error that undermines credibility.

### 3. Francisco Varela description (minor)
**Issue:** Described as "נוירומדען צרפתי-צ'יליאני" (French-Chilean neuroscientist). Varela was Chilean who settled in France in 1986. His primary field was biology/philosophy/cybernetics, not neuroscience per se.
**Fix:** Change to "ביולוג, פילוסוף וחוקר תודעה צ'יליאני" or at minimum "מדען צ'יליאני".
**Severity:** Low.

### 4. Matthieu Ricard's field (minor)
**Issue:** Called "מולקולר-ביולוג" (molecular biologist). His PhD was in molecular genetics, from the Pasteur Institute.
**Fix:** Change to "ביולוג מולקולרי" or "בעל דוקטורט בגנטיקה מולקולרית ממכון פסטר".
**Severity:** Low.

### 5. Galante study - overstated findings (minor-medium)
**Issue:** Blog says LK meditation "מפחיתה דיכאון וחרדה" (reduces depression and anxiety). The meta-analysis found moderate effects against passive controls, but effects were smaller or non-significant against active controls. This is a slight overstatement.
**Fix:** Soften to "הראתה שיפור במצבי דיכאון וחרדה" or add qualifier "בהשוואה לקבוצות שלא תרגלו".
**Severity:** Medium - this is the kind of overstatement that Gatekeeper lessons learned specifically warns about.

### 6. 111-trial meta-analysis - missing attribution (minor)
**Issue:** The meta-analysis of 111 RCTs is mentioned without authors or year. This study was published in 2023 (Health Psychology Review), which is much more recent than the surrounding citations (2004-2015). Not attributing it looks odd.
**Fix:** Either remove the specific "111" number and say "מטא-אנליזה מקיפה של עשרות ניסויים מבוקרים", or add the citation (it's not in the sources list either).
**Severity:** Low-medium. If keeping, must add to sources list.
